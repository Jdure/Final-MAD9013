Keep Things Green

Keep Things Green, is a environmental company founded in Port-au-Prince. Our mission, is to provide devasted areas a chance to re-build in a environmentaly friendly way.

Features

Our main objective, is to help underdevelop countries that will need intervention in case of disasters. Our brand aims, to help clean and rebuild those areas while creating a safe and greener future.

Locations

We are based in Port-au-Prince, Haiti; Ottawa, Canada; Tokyo, Japan; Caracas, Venezuela.

Humanitarian Efforts

Besides rebuilding, with the colloboration of the UN, UNICEF and The Red Cross, we tend to those that have been injured during the disaster.
